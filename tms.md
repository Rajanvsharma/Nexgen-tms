1. Email Integration & Automatic Quotation Creation
•	Integrate TMS with company email accounts.
•	Automatically read customer quote requests from email.
•	Create quotation entries automatically inside TMS.
•	Capture customer name, pickup, delivery, commodity, weight, equipment, dates, rates, and special instructions.
•	Quotation workflow: Pending → Approved → Load Creation.
•	Auto-generate Load Number / PRO Number.
2. Load Board Integrations
•	Direct integrations with DAT, Truckstop, BulkLoads, and future load boards.
•	One-click posting to multiple boards.
•	Automatic updates/removal of load postings.
•	Track posting status.
3. AI Screenshot & OCR Processing
•	Upload screenshots, PDFs, WhatsApp images, or text screenshots.
•	AI/OCR should extract load details automatically.
•	Automatically create quotation or load from extracted data.
•	Allow users to review/edit before saving.
4. User Access Control
•	Role-based permissions.
•	Admin can access all customers, loads, reports, and users.
•	Brokers/users can only view their own customers and loads.
•	All users can view carrier history and lane history.
5. Carrier Database & Lane History
•	Maintain carrier profiles with MC, DOT, insurance, compliance docs, lane history, rates, and performance.
•	Track service quality, detention issues, TONU history, and payment behavior.
•	Search by lane, carrier name, MC number, equipment, and region.
6. Internal Announcement & Notification System
•	Admins, compliance, and accounting can post announcements.
•	Notifications should appear on dashboard and notification bar.
•	Track read/unread status and timestamps.
7. Dispatch & Operations Features
•	Drag-and-drop dispatch board.
•	Driver assignment.
•	Real-time shipment status updates.
•	Load duplication detection.
8. Customer & Carrier Portals
•	Customer portal for tracking loads, invoices, and quote approvals.
•	Carrier portal for POD uploads, invoice submission, and tracking updates.
9. Accounting & Billing Module
•	Invoice generation.
•	Carrier payment scheduling.
•	QuickBooks integration.
•	Aging reports.
•	Factoring support.
10. Compliance Module
•	Insurance monitoring.
•	Authority verification.
•	Safety monitoring.
•	Expiry alerts for insurance, W9, and authority.
11. Tracking Integrations
•	Integrate with MacroPoint, Trucker Tools, Project44, and FourKites.
•	Real-time GPS tracking and ETA alerts.
•	Automated tracking updates.
12. AI Features & Automation
•	AI rate recommendations.
•	Automatic margin protection alerts.
•	Smart carrier matching.
•	AI assistant/chat commands inside TMS.
•	AI email generation for quotations and follow-ups.
•	Workflow automation builder.
13. Fraud Detection
•	Detect double brokering and suspicious carrier activity.
•	Integrate with Highway, RMIS, Carrier411, SaferWatch, and FMCSA.
•	Flag suspicious bank changes and fake identities.
14. Carrier Scorecard System
•	Track on-time pickup/delivery.
•	Tracking compliance.
•	Claims history.
•	Service ratings and performance analytics.
15. Communication Tracking
•	Automatically save emails, notes, SMS, and attachments under each load/customer/carrier.
16. Digital Documents & Signatures
•	Auto-generate rate confirmations, BOLs, invoices, and carrier packets.
•	Support electronic signatures for rate confirmations.
17. KPI & Reporting Dashboard
•	Track active loads, revenue, margins, broker performance, carrier utilization, and customer profitability.
18. Security Requirements
•	Role-based access control.
•	Audit logs.
•	Two-factor authentication.
•	Device/IP tracking.
•	Cloud backup and disaster recovery.
19. Mobile Compatibility
•	Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
•	Optional Android/iPhone mobile app.
20. Future Scalability
•	Support multi-office and multi-team operations.
•	API-first architecture.
•	Cloud hosting.
•	Modular development for future upgrades.
